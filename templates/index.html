DigitaLiteracy

Digital literacy is the shield against cybercrime, empowering individuals to detect threats and navigate the digital world safely. In an era of rising cyberattacks, awareness of phishing, malware, and data breaches is the key to safeguarding personal information. Strong passwords, secure browsing, and two-factor authentication are not just practices but essential defenses. A cyber-aware society is a safer society—where knowledge becomes the best weapon against digital threats.

Self Safety

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and OTP: Activate 2FA on your accounts to add an extra layer of security.

Software Security

  • Install Antivirus Software: Protect your devices from malware, viruses, and ransomware with trusted antivirus software.

National Portal Help

  • National Portal: Report crimes like cyberbullying, and child sexual abuse material (CSAM) via the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal.
